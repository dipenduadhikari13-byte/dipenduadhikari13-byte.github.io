Why These Tools Exist

Image and PDF utilities brought straight to your browser — no download, no login, no ads, zero telemetry. Whether you're a student applying for exams, a small business owner managing documents, or just someone who needs to quickly convert a PDF or resize a photo without uploading it to some random website — these tools are built for you.

The internet is full of "free" tools that are actually harvesting your documents, slapping watermarks, or forcing you through pages of ads. Simple Tools is different. Everything processes right inside your browser and gets deleted afterwards. The maintainer collects nothing.

🛠️ What You'll Find Here

  • Image Resizer & Utilities — Powered by ImageMagick, handle all your JPG/PNG needs
  • PDF Tools — Merge, split, compress using Python's best PDF libraries
  • Doc Tools — Compress and modify DOCX files for job applications and more
  • Image Tools — Quick image modifications with PDF interoperability
  • TokenForge — Custom token generator built for a friend in need

⚠️ Important Notes

The backend (Streamlit Community Cloud) may have separate privacy policies — review Streamlit's privacy policy here. Since these apps run on Streamlit's free tier and aren't hammered by thousands of users, they may go into hibernation. If you see a weird emoji and a "Get this app back up" button — please press it and wait a moment. It wakes up!
